Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

CONTROLS:
D, A - move left/right
W, S - move forwards/backwards
SPACE, CTRL - move +/- along the y axis

Left Click - Place block immediately in front of camera
Middle Click/Right Click - Delete block immediately in front of camera
Note: You cannot delete the blocks pre-loaded into the level!
